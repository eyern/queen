A Little Story About Queen's Pizza

After a fatal eye injury, with savings of 200k Peninah Ntong'ondu resulted in baking pizzas from her one bedroom house in Buruburu and delivering on foot around the estate and its environs. With hard work, resilience and the huge demand of the product, Peninah participated on the KCB lions den show in June 2018 where she landed a 3.5M deal from Kris Senanu that enabled her to open her first outlet on August 1st 2019.
